Samosas Recipe

Description :

Use your air fryer to make Indian samosas - this version is vegetarian with potatoes and peas but you can cook any samosa in the air fryer.

Ingridients :

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 12 ounces yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 24 wonton wrappers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, plus more for garnish
  • 2/3 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps :

  1. Add potatoes to a small saucepan and cover with salted water; bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes or until fork-tender. Drain and transfer potatoes to a bowl; mash until mostly smooth with a few small chunks remaining.
  2. Arrange wonton wrappers on a clean work surface; evenly spoon filling into centers of wontonwrappers. Lightly wet borders of wrappers with water. Fold two corners together to form triangles. Press edges to seal. Coat both sides of samosas with cooking spray.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat air fryer basket with cooking spray.Working in batches if needed, arrange samosas in an even layer in the basket. Cook, turning once halfway through, until samosas are golden brown and crispy, about 8 minutes.

  5. Serve samosas with cilantro-yogurt sauce. Garnish with additional cilantro.
